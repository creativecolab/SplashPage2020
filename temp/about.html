What is D4SD?

 

D4SD = Design For San Diego

A Civic Design Competition

 

D4SD is a civic competition bringing together innovators, domain experts, design mentors, business and civic leaders, and vulnerable populations to address complex issues facing San Diego with a human-centered design approach.
